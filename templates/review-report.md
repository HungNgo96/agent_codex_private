# Review Report

## Disposition

APPROVED or CHANGES_REQUESTED

## Findings

Write findings in severity order. If there are no findings, write: `No findings.`

### Finding 1

Severity: High | Medium | Low
File: `path/to/file:line`
Issue: Describe the problem.
Impact: Describe what can break.
Recommendation: Describe the concrete fix or next check.

## Questions

List questions or assumptions that need confirmation. For approved or no-findings reports, write `None.` when there are no open questions.

## Verification Gaps

List checks that were not run or coverage that is missing. For approved or no-findings reports, write `None.` when there are no known gaps.

## Summary

Briefly summarize overall risk after the findings. For approved or no-findings reports, do not use a vague placeholder; state the residual risk explicitly, even if it is low.
